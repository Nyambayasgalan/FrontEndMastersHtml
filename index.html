How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich?

  1. Get some peanut butter, jelly and bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on the one side of bread and jelly on the other.
  3. Put the peanut butter and jelly together, with the bread side facing out.
  4. Eat and enjoy!
